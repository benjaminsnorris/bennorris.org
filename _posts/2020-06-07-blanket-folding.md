My wife folded the 8yo’s blanket and the edges were uneven. 

8yo, as he saw the blanket: “Ohh... Can we just start over?”

Wife, tiny sigh: “Ok.”

8yo: “I’m not saying your work is bad. I’m just saying we do better together.”
