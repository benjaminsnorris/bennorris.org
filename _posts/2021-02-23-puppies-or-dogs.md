7yo: “I like puppies better than dogs.”

Me: “Do you know what happens to puppies?”

7yo: “What?”

Me: “They become dogs.”

7yo: <dramatic gasp>

Me: “Puppies are baby dogs.”

7yo: “Yeah, but they’re mostly puppies.”
